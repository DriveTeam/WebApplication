Event Rules & Guidelines

EVENT RULES & GUIDELINES APPLICABLE TO ALL PARTICIPANTS

Rain Policy and Cancellation of Shows

Shows will not be canceled due to showers or thunderstorms. Only in instances of dangerous, inclement weather (such as a hurricane, tropical storm or tornado warnings) will a performance be cancelled.

Devices for rent

If you want a charger, listening device or other stuff, these devices are available at the Reception desk Please take along your RFID to rent one. And Please don't forget to return otherwise you need pay a fine.

Refreshments

We stand offers refreshments and other supplies for picnics. Purchasing concessions at the store supports the free performances.

Camping

You can only enter the camping after activating your ticket at the entrance of the event. And if you are not the one made the reservation, you must come along with the person who reserved it.

No Smoking

Smoking is prohibited in the seated area as well as in the camping.

Pets

All pets must be on a leash at all times and accompanied by their owner. Large animals are not permitted without the prior consent of Department of Public Safety.

No damage

Please take care of all the public facilities inside the event area and the camping.
